What you're looking at

I took your current site and rebuilt it into three different visual styles so you can see the options side by side and get a feel for what fits Spartacus best. All three use your real content, logo, and photos, same sections, three distinct personalities.

Nothing here is set in stone. Think of this as a starting point, not a finished product. We can mix pieces from different styles, change colors, fonts, layout, wording, add pages, whatever you have in mind. Pick a favorite (or the parts you like from each) and we'll take it from there.
