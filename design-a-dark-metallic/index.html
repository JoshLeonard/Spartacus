Our Story

Built on Service. Driven to Protect.

Spartacus Protection & Consulting was founded on a commitment to protecting the communities we serve. Backed by more than 15 years of combined law enforcement and military experience, we deliver trusted, high-quality security solutions tailored to schools and faith-based organizations throughout Indiana.

Enhance safety. Strengthen preparedness. Provide peace of mind.

Our mission is simple: to enhance safety, strengthen preparedness, and provide peace of mind through proactive, professional protection strategies. Through unmatched expertise and a client-focused approach, we build confidence and support the long-term goals of the organizations we serve.

We believe strong partnerships are the foundation of effective protection, and together, we can create safer, more secure communities for the future.

Meet the Team

The People Behind the Shield

David “Dino” Farrell

President / CEO

With more than 15 years of combined military and law enforcement experience, Dino has built a career centered on leadership, service, and community protection. A respected leader within Indiana's School Resource Officer community, he is passionate about creating safer schools, mentoring students, and building strong relationships between youth and law enforcement. He serves as Vice President of the Indiana School Resource Officers Association Foundation Board.

Amy Nickol

Director of Operations

Amy brings 15 years of experience in public education and a strong commitment to safe, supportive, student-centered environments. A certified Crime Prevention Through Environmental Design (CPTED) practitioner and Indiana School Safety Specialist, she stays current through both INSROA and NASRO. Through coaching and mentoring roles, she is passionate about building confidence and helping students grow on and off the court.
