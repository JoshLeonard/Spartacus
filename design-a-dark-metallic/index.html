Security Assessments

Know Your Vulnerabilities

01Building Interior

Systematic evaluation of a building's interior areas to identify vulnerabilities that could be exploited to gain access, cause harm, or disrupt operations.

02Building Exterior

Evaluation of the outside areas of a building, including entry points, lighting, landscaping, and signage, to find weaknesses that pose safety risks.

03Access Controls

Structured review of the controls that govern who can enter your facility and systems, and how effectively they limit unauthorized access.

04Security Equipment

Systematic evaluation of all physical security devices and systems to ensure they are effective, properly maintained, and aligned with your needs.

05Communication Systems

Detailed evaluation of the measures protecting your communication systems for secure, reliable, and resilient communications day-to-day and in a crisis.

06Reporting Procedures

Evaluation of how security concerns are reported and handled, ensuring clear, accessible processes to address threats before they escalate.

07Cybersecurity

Comprehensive evaluation of your digital infrastructure, policies, and practices to identify vulnerabilities and strengthen defenses.

08Emergency Operations Plan

Comprehensive review of how well your emergency plan integrates security measures before, during, and after an incident.

09Threat Assessment Team

Evaluation and support for a multidisciplinary team that identifies, assesses, and manages potential threats before they escalate.

Safety Training

Prepared for the Moment That Matters

Active Shooter Training

Equips participants with the knowledge, mindset, and practical skills to respond decisively and safely during an active threat, emphasizing situational awareness, survival tactics, and coordinated response.

Participants learn to
  • Recognize warning signs of potential violence
  • Respond using the Run, Hide, Fight (or ALICE) model
  • Communicate under stress and alert law enforcement
  • Coordinate with first responders on arrival
  • Develop situational awareness in everyday settings

Reunification Training

Prepares staff, security personnel, and administrators to safely reunite students, employees, or congregants with their families after an emergency, minimizing confusion, distress, and risk.

Core components
  • Planning & preparation
  • Roles & responsibilities
  • Process & procedure
  • Scenario-based exercises
  • Communication & documentation

Threat Assessment Team Training

Helps organizations establish or strengthen a multidisciplinary team that identifies, assesses, and manages concerning behaviors and credible threats.

Focus areas
  • Identifying and evaluating concerning behavior
  • Coordinated intervention and mitigation
  • Prevention before threats escalate
  • Alignment with your Emergency Operations Plan
