“ Our Story

Built on service.
Driven to protect.

Spartacus Protection & Consulting was founded on a commitment to protecting the communities we serve. Backed by more than 15 years of combined law enforcement and military experience, we deliver trusted, high-quality security solutions tailored to schools and faith-based organizations throughout Indiana.

Our mission is simple: to enhance safety, strengthen preparedness, and provide peace of mind through proactive, professional protection strategies. Through unmatched expertise and a client-focused approach, we build confidence and support the long-term goals of the organizations we serve.

We believe strong partnerships are the foundation of effective protection, and together, we can create safer, more secure communities for the future.

David “Dino” Farrell

President / CEO

With more than 15 years of combined military and law enforcement experience, Dino has built a career centered on leadership, service, and community protection. Amy Nickol

Director of Operations

Amy brings 15 years of experience in public education and a strong commitment to safe, supportive, student-centered environments. A certified CPTED practitioner and Indiana School Safety Specialist active in both INSROA and NASRO, she is passionate about mentoring young people and building confidence on and off the court.
